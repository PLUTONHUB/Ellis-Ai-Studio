# Review Request Messages

Ask for honest feedback only. Do not offer incentives, request only positive reviews, or pressure customers.

## Email

**Subject:** How was your experience with [Business Name]?

Hi [First Name],

Thank you for choosing [Business Name] for [service or project]. We value your feedback and would appreciate an honest Google review if you have a moment.

[Approved review link]

Your feedback helps us improve and helps other customers make informed decisions.

Thank you,
[Name / Business Name]

## SMS

Thanks for choosing [Business Name] for [service or project]. We’d value your honest feedback: [approved review link]. Thank you!
