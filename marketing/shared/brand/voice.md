# Ellis AI Studio Brand Voice

Ellis AI Studio sounds practical, clear, and capable. We explain complex work without jargon and focus on the real-world result for service businesses.

## Voice traits

- **Grounded:** Use concrete language, examples, and evidence.
- **Helpful:** Teach before selling; make the next step understandable.
- **Confident, not inflated:** State what is known and avoid unsupported superlatives.
- **Human:** Write like an experienced collaborator, not a faceless platform.

Prefer “make booking easier” over “revolutionize your operations.”
