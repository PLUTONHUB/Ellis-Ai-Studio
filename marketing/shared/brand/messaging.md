# Messaging Hierarchy

## Primary message

Ellis AI Studio builds practical websites, automations, AI tools, and custom systems for service businesses.

## Supporting messages

1. Make it easier for customers to find, understand, and choose a service.
2. Improve operational efficiency with clear, useful systems.
3. Build around the business and its customers instead of forcing a generic workflow.

## Proof guidance

Use approved project details, product demonstrations, customer-safe stories, and verified outcomes. Never invent performance claims, testimonials, timelines, or metrics.
