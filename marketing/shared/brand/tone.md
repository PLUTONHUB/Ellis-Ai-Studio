# Tone

## Default

Clear, warm, direct, and calm.

## Adapt by context

- **Educational:** Patient and specific.
- **Launches:** Energized but factual.
- **Founder updates:** Candid and reflective.
- **Customer stories:** Respectful, outcome-led, and permission-aware.
- **Problem response:** Empathetic and action-oriented.

Avoid hype, fear-based urgency, unexplained acronyms, and claims that cannot be verified.
