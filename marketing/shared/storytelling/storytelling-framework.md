# Storytelling Framework

1. **Context:** What is the customer, workflow, or situation?
2. **Friction:** What made the previous experience difficult?
3. **Change:** What did Ellis AI Studio build, improve, or learn?
4. **Outcome:** What is easier, clearer, or more useful now?
5. **Next step:** What should the audience do with this insight?

Use the smallest version that fits the platform. A short video may show only friction, change, and CTA; a case study can use all five.
