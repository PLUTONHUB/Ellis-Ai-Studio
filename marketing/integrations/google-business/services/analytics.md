# Analytics Service

Retrieve available Business Profile performance data on an approved schedule and write source-linked records to `marketing/analytics/platform-metrics/`. Track, where the authorized API and location provide them:

- Views / impressions
- Searches
- Calls
- Website clicks
- Direction requests

Forward summarized, date-bounded records to `marketing/intelligence/content-intelligence/` and `marketing/intelligence/insights/` for analysis. Do not infer unavailable metrics or use small samples to make broad recommendations.
