# Business Information Service

Retrieve and cache a permission-scoped, timestamped snapshot of the selected Business Profile location:

- Business name
- Address
- Hours
- Phone
- Website
- Categories

Normalize location identifiers and field values without overwriting source data. Record retrieval time, account/location IDs, requested fields, and API response version. Business information may inform drafts and local SEO ideas, but reviewers must confirm it before publication.
