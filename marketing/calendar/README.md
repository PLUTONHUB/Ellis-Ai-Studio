# Marketing Calendar

The calendar turns approved content and campaigns into a predictable publishing plan. Use `marketing/shared/` for message choices, then place only reviewed, scheduled content here.

- `weekly/` — Working schedule with post owner, channel, source, status, and review date.
- `monthly/` — Cadence plan, goals, themes, and capacity view.
- `launch-calendar/` — Milestone plan for launch sequencing across channels.

## Cadence and review

Plan the month first, refine the upcoming week, and review the queue before recording or publishing. Each scheduled item should link to its content-library record, campaign when applicable, platform draft, owner, publish window, and approval status.
