# Content Format Blocks

## Long Caption

Hook, context, 2–4 short value-led paragraphs, clear CTA, and 3–8 approved hashtags.

## Short Caption

One concise insight, one supporting sentence, one CTA, and 3–5 approved hashtags.

## Carousel Outline

Cover promise, 3–7 value slides, and a closing slide with a save/share CTA.

## Reel Script

0–3 second hook, concise visual beats, on-screen text, voiceover, and end-card CTA.

## Story Text

One idea per frame, plain language, an optional poll/question sticker, and a final action.
