# Hook Variations

- “I wish I knew this before [task].”
- “Here’s how we turned [problem] into [outcome].”
- “Watch what happens when [change].”
- “The biggest mistake in [topic] is [myth].”
- “We built this because [pain point].”
- “Before you [action], try this.”

Adapt the hook to the actual content; do not make claims the video cannot support.
