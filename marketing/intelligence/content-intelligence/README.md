# Content Intelligence

Track content-library IDs and source events to identify:

- Best-performing content by outcome, channel, hook, CTA, and format
- Evergreen content worth recurring or repurposing
- Seasonal content tied to predictable moments
- Platform-specific recommendations based on comparable posts
- Repurposing opportunities across formats and channels

Record the metric, date range, comparison set, source link, confidence, and recommended next action. Send approved findings to campaigns, scheduling, and repurpose workflows.
