# Sales Asset Library

## Shared outreach and discovery toolkit

**Cold email:** `Subject: A growth opportunity for {Business}. I reviewed your public customer journey and identified opportunities to improve {priority outcome}. Ellis builds practical growth systems for {Industry} businesses. Would a short Growth Strategy Call be useful?`

**LinkedIn / Facebook / Instagram:** `I reviewed {Business}'s public presence and see a practical opportunity to strengthen {priority outcome}. Open to a short business-intelligence review?`

**Call / voicemail:** `Hi, this is Ellis AI Studio. I prepared a concise view of {Business}'s public growth opportunities. I’d welcome the chance to share the most relevant one.`

**Discovery agenda:** goals and capacity → current acquisition/conversion path → evidence and constraints → priority system → agreed next step.

**Objection responses:** “We already market” → Ellis improves the system around existing demand. “What is the ROI?” → agree a baseline and measurable business-impact assumptions. “We are busy” → prioritize systems that reduce operational load.

**Follow-up:** 24 hours: evidence summary and one question; 3 days: relevant opportunity; 7 days: recommended system; 14 days: business-outcome example; 30 days: respectful close-the-loop invitation.

**Proposal cover:** `Ellis recommends a growth partnership focused on {Business Outcome}. This proposal describes the system, implementation path, measurable checkpoints, and next decision—not a technology purchase.`

## Creative direction templates

Use the canonical [Brand Design System](../foundation/BRAND_DESIGN_SYSTEM.md) and [Design Prompt Library](../foundation/DESIGN_PROMPT_LIBRARY.md). Parameterize `{industry}`, `{outcome}`, `{audience}`, `{cta}`.

- Instagram/Facebook: `Premium minimal {industry} growth opportunity graphic; outcome {outcome}; audience {audience}; CTA {cta}; editorial hierarchy, warm ivory and deep ink, no generic AI imagery.`
- LinkedIn carousel: `Six-slide executive carousel for {industry}: challenge, business intelligence, system, expected impact, roadmap, CTA.`
- Hero/proposal cover: `Premium {industry} business-growth cover showing trusted service, clear outcomes, restrained diagrammatic detail.`
- Case study/infographic/before-after/ad: `Evidence-led {industry} comparison showing current customer journey, improved growth system, expected business impact, and CTA.`
