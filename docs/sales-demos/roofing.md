# Roofing Demo Package

## Executive summary and intelligence

Roofing businesses win when urgent demand reaches a trusted local expert quickly. Common opportunities are clearer inspection/estimate paths, seasonal/storm content, review proof, faster lead response, and follow-up for unconverted estimates. This is a demonstration package; validate every claim against a real prospect’s public evidence.

## Growth opportunity report

**Customer acquisition:** local service-area pages, storm-readiness content, Google Business visibility, proof-led project galleries. **Lead conversion:** inspection CTA, mobile-first estimate request, response-time expectation, financing clarity. **Operational efficiency:** lead routing, estimate follow-up, job-status communication. **Recommended systems:** Website Systems, Lead Recovery System, Content Engine, Business Intelligence. **Estimated impact:** quantify after discovery from lead volume, close rate, average project value, and response baseline.

## 90-day roadmap and journey

Days 1–30: business intelligence, baseline, conversion-path plan. Days 31–60: launch inspection journey, response workflow, proof assets. Days 61–90: measure, optimize, and expand winning acquisition sources. Journey: local search/referral → service/storm page → inspection request → rapid response → estimate → follow-up → booked project → review/referral.

## Discovery, proposal, FAQ, and CTA

Ask about service areas, storm/seasonality, lead sources, estimate-to-close rate, response ownership, capacity, financing, and review process. Proposal preview: Growth System for qualified inspection demand, conversion, and follow-up; timeline and investment are confirmed after discovery. FAQ: “Will this replace our team?” No—systems make the team more responsive. “How is impact measured?” Baselines, conversion checkpoints, and booked revenue. **CTA:** Book a Growth Strategy Call.

## Landing-page and portfolio specification

**Hero:** `Turn local roofing demand into booked, trusted projects.` Pain points: missed calls, slow estimates, weak proof. Process: Intelligence → Strategy → System → Optimization. Trust: verified licenses, reviews, project evidence only. SEO: `{city} roofing marketing`, `roofing lead conversion`, `roofing estimate follow-up`. Demo portfolio: challenge—demand not consistently converted; solution—inspection journey, proof, and follow-up; expected impact—more qualified inspections and clearer close-rate measurement; technology—website, CRM workflow, analytics; lesson—speed and trust must work together.
