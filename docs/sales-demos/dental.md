# Dental Demo Package

## Executive summary and intelligence

Dental practices grow through patient trust, clear care paths, and a low-effort first appointment experience. Common opportunities are new-patient conversion, service education, review proof, recall/reactivation, and intake efficiency.

## Growth opportunity report

**Customer acquisition:** local patient-intent pages, educational content, review visibility, referral reinforcement. **Lead conversion:** insurance and new-patient clarity, booking path, reassurance, treatment-plan follow-up. **Operational efficiency:** digital intake, recall sequences, missed-call response, treatment acceptance context. **Recommended systems:** Website Systems, Lead Recovery System, Content Engine, AI Automation Systems. **Estimated impact:** validate from new-patient inquiries, appointment conversion, treatment acceptance, and lifetime patient value.

## 90-day roadmap and journey

Days 1–30: business intelligence and patient-path assessment. Days 31–60: new-patient journey, trust content, response workflow. Days 61–90: optimize recall and service-line campaigns. Journey: search/referral → care/service page → appointment request → reassurance/confirmation → first visit → treatment/recall → review/referral.

## Discovery, proposal, FAQ, and CTA

Ask about desired patient mix, new-patient capacity, insurance questions, booking abandonment, treatment acceptance, recall process, and reviews. Proposal preview: Dental Growth System that strengthens patient acquisition, appointment conversion, and front-desk efficiency. FAQ: “Will this sound impersonal?” The system is designed to make human care easier to access. **CTA:** Explore Growth Opportunities.

## Landing-page and portfolio specification

**Hero:** `Make it easier for the right patients to choose your practice.` Pain points: unclear first visit, weak proof, unconverted treatment interest. SEO: `{city} dental marketing`, `new patient conversion`, `dental appointment booking`. Demo portfolio: challenge—patient intent did not consistently become booked care; solution—clear service paths, trust evidence, intelligent follow-up; expected impact—more confident booking and measurable patient acquisition; technology—website, CRM workflow, analytics; lesson—clarity is part of patient care.
