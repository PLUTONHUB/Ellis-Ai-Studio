# HVAC Demo Package

## Executive summary and intelligence

HVAC buyers need confidence quickly, especially during weather-driven urgency. Common opportunities are emergency and maintenance journeys, service-plan positioning, trusted proof, booking clarity, and consistent follow-up.

## Growth opportunity report

**Customer acquisition:** seasonal education, local service visibility, maintenance-plan content, review amplification. **Lead conversion:** emergency/repair/replacement routing, clear availability, financing/estimate paths. **Operational efficiency:** call capture, after-hours intake, membership renewal, technician-ready context. **Recommended systems:** Growth Systems, Website Systems, Lead Recovery System, AI Automation Systems. **Estimated impact:** validate from call volume, booking rate, plan value, and response-time baseline.

## 90-day roadmap and journey

Days 1–30: intelligence and customer-journey baseline. Days 31–60: service/booking system and response workflow. Days 61–90: optimize maintenance and replacement campaigns. Journey: search/referral → urgent or planned service path → booking → confirmation → technician visit → plan/replacement recommendation → review/referral.

## Discovery, proposal, FAQ, and CTA

Ask about peak seasons, after-hours coverage, maintenance-plan adoption, lead source, booking capacity, replacement funnel, and response SLA. Proposal preview: HVAC Growth System that increases qualified bookings and supports efficient service operations. FAQ: “Can automation answer customers?” It can support approved intake and routing, with human accountability. **CTA:** Discuss Your Business Goals.

## Landing-page and portfolio specification

**Hero:** `Build a clearer path from HVAC demand to booked service.` Pain points: urgent calls, seasonal swings, manual follow-up. SEO: `{city} HVAC lead generation`, `HVAC booking conversion`, `HVAC maintenance marketing`. Demo portfolio: challenge—valuable demand lost between inquiry and booking; solution—service pathways, responsive follow-up, lifecycle content; expected impact—higher booking confidence and plan adoption; technology—website, automation, analytics; lesson—availability must be clear before a customer calls.
