# Service Packages

Ellis sells the reusable service blueprints listed in [Service Blueprints](service-blueprints.md): Growth Systems, Lead Recovery System, Website Systems, AI Automation Systems, Content Engine, Growth Engine, and Business Intelligence. A package is a versioned combination of scope, outcomes, delivery cadence, acceptance criteria, and price; it is not a custom collection of hours.

Operations owns the canonical service/package definitions. Qualification and expansion use [Ideal Client Profile](IDEAL_CLIENT_PROFILE.md) and [Pricing Strategy](PRICING_STRATEGY.md).
