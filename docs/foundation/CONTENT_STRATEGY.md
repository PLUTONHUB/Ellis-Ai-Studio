# Content Strategy

Content exists to earn attention from qualified buyers, demonstrate useful expertise, and create evidence for a sales conversation. Every asset maps to an ICP pain point, a business objective, a channel hypothesis, an approved brand profile, and a measurable call to action. Repurpose canonical content assets across channels rather than recreating ideas or claims.

Content ownership is defined in the [Domain Architecture](../ellis-core-platform/domain-architecture.md); performance learning uses [Platform Flywheels](platform-flywheels.md).
