# Ideal Client Profile

An ideal client has a proven service, clear customer value, sufficient lead volume or growth ambition, a decision-maker who can act, and willingness to measure outcomes. They have a costly constraint in acquisition, conversion, delivery, or follow-up that a reusable system can address.

Disqualifiers: no defined offer, inability to serve added demand, no decision-maker access, or expectation of unmeasured commodity work. Sales uses these criteria in [Sales Playbook](SALES_PLAYBOOK.md).
