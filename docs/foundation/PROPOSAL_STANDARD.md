# Proposal Standard

Every proposal includes the client objective, current-state evidence, recommended package/version, deliverables, exclusions, client responsibilities, timeline, investment/payment schedule, success measures, assumptions, risks, change control, and acceptance next step. Proposals reference canonical service definitions; they do not redefine them.

Follow [Pricing Strategy](PRICING_STRATEGY.md) and hand accepted work to [Client Onboarding](CLIENT_ONBOARDING.md).
