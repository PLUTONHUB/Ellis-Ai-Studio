# Revenue Engine

Revenue Engine prepares a founder-reviewed acquisition package from a single business intake. It consumes the canonical Prospect Intelligence Report, then produces discovery preparation, proposal draft, channel-ready outreach drafts, follow-up timeline, and explained recommendations. It never sends communications.

Workflow: Intake → Prospect Intelligence → Business Intelligence Report → Growth Opportunity Analysis → Recommended Services → Discovery Preparation → Proposal Generation → Follow-Up Planning.

Every artifact is an internal draft with source-report provenance. This preserves founder time for conversations and persuasion.
