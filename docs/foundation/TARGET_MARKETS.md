# Target Markets

Primary markets are established service businesses with high-value customer relationships, repeatable lead flows, measurable sales outcomes, and operational capacity to act on qualified demand. Initial focus favors local and regional professional, home, health/wellness, and specialist services where digital presence and follow-up materially affect revenue.

Qualification criteria are authoritative in [Ideal Client Profile](IDEAL_CLIENT_PROFILE.md). Market research is owned by the Business Intelligence domain.
