# Service Blueprints

The commercial package authority is [Service Packages](SERVICE_PACKAGES.md). Each blueprint below is a reusable outcome system; exact scope and price are versioned package records.

| Service | Objective / ideal client / pain | Deliverables and systems | Automation/assets | KPI, success, expansion |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Growth Systems | Create measurable acquisition for ICP-fit service businesses with inconsistent demand | strategy, campaign plan, funnel measurement; Intelligence/Campaign/Analytics | templates, experiments, dashboards | qualified pipeline and conversion; expand to channel optimization |
| Lead Recovery System | Recover high-intent leads for businesses with slow or inconsistent follow-up | lead routing, follow-up workflow, reporting; CRM/Outreach/Automation | sequences, qualification rules | response time, recovered revenue; expand to lifecycle nurture |
| Website Systems | Make the website a clear conversion asset for businesses with weak digital trust or conversion | digital presence assessment, conversion plan, website improvements, measurement; Website Intelligence/Analytics | assessment templates, components | conversion and lead quality; expand to personalization |
| AI Automation Systems | Remove repeatable operational work where data/approval constraints are understood | workflow, controls, monitoring; Automation/Knowledge/QA | reusable workflows, contracts | time saved, error rate; expand to agent-assisted operations |
| Content Engine | Produce repeatable buyer-relevant content for businesses lacking consistency | content strategy, asset pipeline, publishing; Content/Copy/Creative | prompts, templates, asset library | qualified engagement, production cycle time; expand to campaigns |
| Growth Engine | Operate the continuous Ellis acquisition system | monitoring through learning loop; all growth domains | experiments, channel adapters | qualified pipeline/CPL; expand to new market segments |
| Business Intelligence | Improve decisions for businesses lacking clear market/customer insight | research, assessments, opportunity brief; Business/Website Intelligence | research frameworks, benchmarks | decision adoption, opportunity conversion; expand to ongoing advisory |

All services use the delivery process in [Client Delivery Standard](CLIENT_DELIVERY_STANDARD.md), pass [Business Validation Gates](../architecture/business-validation-gates.md), and require a new ADR when changing platform architecture.
