# Pricing Strategy

Price for expected business value, complexity, risk, speed, and operating responsibility—not activity alone. Use a paid discovery when scope/outcome uncertainty is material; price implementation as a defined system; price ongoing optimization as a recurring operating service. Require an approved scope, assumptions, success measure, change-control rule, and payment schedule before delivery.

The authoritative package scope is [Service Packages](SERVICE_PACKAGES.md); proposal structure is [Proposal Standard](PROPOSAL_STANDARD.md).
