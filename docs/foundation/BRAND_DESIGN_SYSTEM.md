# Brand Design System

This is the visual single source of truth for Ellis assets. Use premium editorial sans typography; generous 8px spacing rhythm; 12-column desktop / 4-column mobile grid; deep ink primary, warm ivory surface, muted stone secondary, and a restrained confident accent. Favor generous negative space, clear hierarchy, precise typography, real business environments, and subtle texture.

Photography is composed, natural, and human/business-led. Illustration is restrained diagrammatic geometry. Avoid robots, brains, neon circuitry, stock “AI” visuals, clutter, and unsupported outcome claims. Icons are simple outlined forms. CTAs are compact high-contrast labels with a clear next action. Motion, when added, is calm: fade, translate, and purposeful sequencing only.

Brand voice authority remains [Brand Positioning](BRAND_POSITIONING.md); creative prompt authority is [Design Prompt Library](DESIGN_PROMPT_LIBRARY.md).
