# Growth Engine

The Growth Engine is Ellis's first internal product: it monitors Ellis, detects changes, produces approved campaigns and assets, publishes through channel adapters, measures results, and recommends the next experiment. It exists to create qualified pipeline while people focus on selling, discovery, delivery, and relationships.

The complete lifecycle and measures are authoritative in [Business Operating System](../ellis-core-platform/business-operating-system.md); worker responsibilities are in [AI Worker Contracts](ai-worker-contracts.md).
