# Platform Flywheels

| Flywheel | Mission / inputs → outputs | KPIs | Systems and value | Expansion |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Growth | Turn market evidence, brand, services, and content into qualified pipeline → campaigns, conversations, qualified opportunities | qualified pipeline, conversion, cost per qualified lead | Intelligence, Content, Campaign, Publishing, Analytics; creates predictable acquisition | new channels and attribution |
| Delivery | Turn agreed scope, client context, and reusable assets into outcomes → approvals, evidence, case studies | time to value, margin, retention, outcome attainment | CRM, Delivery, Automation, Knowledge; creates better client outcomes and reusable delivery assets | client portal and benchmarked delivery |
| Intelligence | Turn interactions, projects, and analytics into governed evidence → insights, memory, recommendations | evidence coverage, recommendation adoption, measured lift | Knowledge, Analytics, Learning, QA; reduces repeated discovery and improves decisions | secure cross-client pattern learning |
| Platform | Turn repeated validated capabilities into shared contracts/assets → faster, safer releases | reuse rate, lead time, defect rate, cost to serve | shared packages, ADRs, CI/CD, observability; compounds maintainability and competitive advantage | independent products and client modules |

Dependencies and canonical ownership are defined in [Domain Architecture](../ellis-core-platform/domain-architecture.md); the Growth Engine sequence is in [Growth Engine](GROWTH_ENGINE.md).
