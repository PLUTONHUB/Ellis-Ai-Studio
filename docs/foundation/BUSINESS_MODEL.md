# Business Model

Ellis sells productized growth-system services, supported by recurring optimization and reusable platform capabilities. Revenue comes from discovery/strategy, implementation, managed operation, and expansion into additional systems. Margin improves when approved reusable assets, templates, and automations reduce delivery time without reducing quality.

The offers are authoritative in [Service Packages](SERVICE_PACKAGES.md); commercial guardrails are in [Pricing Strategy](PRICING_STRATEGY.md).
