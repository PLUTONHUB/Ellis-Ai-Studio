# Client Delivery Standard

Deliver a defined package through a project plan, accountable owner, evidence baseline, milestone approvals, quality checks, change control, outcome reporting, and closeout. Maintain one project/task record; publish client-ready progress through approved projections. Escalate risks early and document decisions.

Case-study eligibility follows [Case Study Process](CASE_STUDY_PROCESS.md); operating principles are [Core Values](CORE_VALUES.md).
