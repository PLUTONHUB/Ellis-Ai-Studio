# ADR-0008: Ellis AI Studio brand vocabulary

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Ellis AI Studio is an AI systems company. We design, build, and manage AI-powered growth infrastructure for businesses.

Canonical public vocabulary includes AI Systems Engineering, Intelligent Websites, Custom Software, AI Agents, Business Automation, Content Operations, Lead Generation Systems, Operational Intelligence, Scalable Business Infrastructure, AI Integration, and Business Growth Technology.

## Guidance

Public materials describe outcomes in clear business terms: acquiring customers, automating operations, improving decision making, and scaling efficiently. Marketing, product UI, prompts, social content, and SEO use the same vocabulary. Internal implementation details do not define the public offer.

## Success Criteria

Every customer-facing surface communicates one concise, consistent position: Ellis AI Studio engineers AI-powered growth systems for businesses.
