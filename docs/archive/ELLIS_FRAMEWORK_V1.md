# Ellis Framework V1 — Historical Reference Only

## Historical evolution

Earlier Ellis materials used a friction-oriented framework.

## Retirement rationale

That language centered problems rather than Ellis's product: AI-powered Growth Systems delivering customer acquisition, lead conversion, operational efficiency, and measurable business outcomes.

## Lessons retained

Evidence-based diagnosis, prioritization, and specific recommendations remain valuable. The active methodology is the Ellis Business Intelligence Framework.

## Active replacement vocabulary

Use Business Intelligence Report, Growth Opportunity Report, Digital Presence Assessment, Opportunity Score, Business Intelligence Categories, Growth Recommendations, Estimated Business Impact, and Recommended Growth Roadmap.

This document is archival only and must not be used as active sales, product, worker, or prompt guidance.
